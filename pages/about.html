Mission & Vision

Why Ascovita Exists

India has the world's highest rate of micronutrient deficiency — yet most supplement brands charge a premium that keeps quality nutrition out of reach for millions. We founded Ascovita to break that cycle.

From our GMP-certified facility in Anand, Gujarat, we formulate, manufacture, and deliver premium nutraceuticals — effervescent tablets, spirulina, glutathione, biotin and more — at prices that make good health truly accessible.

"From formulation to manufacturing of nutraceutical products — one standard, no shortcuts."

— Ascovita Healthcare Promise
